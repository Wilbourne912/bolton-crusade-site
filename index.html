Our Story


Bolton Crusade Ministry (BCM) was founded by Bishop L. W. Bolton, Jr. nearly 40 years ago. As the Evangelical Arm of the Mississippi National Baptist State Convention, we dedicate our mission to spreading the gospel and building an impactful community of faith across Mississippi and beyond.
